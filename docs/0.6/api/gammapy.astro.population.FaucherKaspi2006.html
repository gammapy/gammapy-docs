FaucherKaspi2006¶

class gammapy.astro.population.FaucherKaspi2006(amplitude=1, r_0=7.04, sigma=1.83, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: astropy.modeling.Fittable1DModel

Radial distribution of the birth surface density of pulsars in the galaxy - Faucher-Giguere & Kaspi 2006.

\[f(r) = A \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi} \sigma} \exp \left(- \frac{(r - r_0)^2}{2 \sigma ^ 2}\right)\]

Reference: http://adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2006ApJ...643..332F (Appendix B)
