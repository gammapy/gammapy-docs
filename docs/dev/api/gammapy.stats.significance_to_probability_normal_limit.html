significance_to_probability_normal_limit¶

gammapy.stats.significance_to_probability_normal_limit(significance, guess=1e-100)[source]¶

Convert significance to tail probability in the limit of small p and large s.

See p_to_s_limit docstring Note: s^2 = u - log(u) can’t be solved analytically.
